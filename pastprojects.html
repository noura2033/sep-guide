SEP Throughout Your 4 Highschool Years

Freshmen Year:

You will start of by getting the basic fundamentals of Software Engineering.
Scratch is a fun program of getting the basic idea of how code works with captivating animation.
Get a sense of how electronics work with Arduino! Arduino is an open-source electronics platform
that allows easy accessability for programmars, designers and artists.

Sophomore Year:

Into your sophomore, you will be introduced to Processing. In Processing you'll learn how to
code within the context of the visual arts.

Junior Year:

As you grow into your junior year, you'll have a lot of basic and advacned knowledge on programming.
Start expressing your creativity with Web Design! Learn languages such as jQuery, Bootstrap
, etc to build your own website. Keep in mind as your High School career continues, you can grow your
knowledge and make you projects more impressive.
